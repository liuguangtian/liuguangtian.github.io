Research Projects

计及持续性可再生能源干旱与季节性电量不平衡联合风险的长期电力系统规划

Long-Term Power System Planning Considering Joint Risks of Persistent Renewable Droughts and Seasonal Energy Imbalances

2025 - Present
  • 针对持续性极端低新能源出力 (ELRG) 与季节性电量不平衡 (SEI) 的联合风险，构建了长周期的电力系统规划框架。
  • Developed a long-term power system planning framework addressing the joint risks of persistent extreme low renewable generation (ELRG) and seasonal electricity imbalance (SEI).
  • 提出并开发了一种新的 ELRG 事件聚类方法。该方法直接提取并筛选代表性的真实事件进行分析，摒弃了传统的统一长度典型片段生成法与事件分类法，精确保留了系统真实的物理特性边界。
  • Proposed a novel ELRG event clustering method. By selecting representative real events rather than generating unified-length typical fragments or relying on event classification, this approach preserves the actual physical characteristics and boundaries of the system.
  • 目前相关研究成果已撰写成文，计划提交至 IEEE 顶级期刊。
  • Target outcome structured into a manuscript, prepared for submission to a top-tier IEEE journal.
